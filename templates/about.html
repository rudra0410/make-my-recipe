About MakeMyRecipe

Discover the story behind our cooking assistant and how we help you create magic in your kitchen.

Our Mission

MakeMyRecipe was born from a simple idea: to help people create delicious Indian meals with whatever ingredients they have at home. We understand that sometimes you're missing that one ingredient from a recipe, or maybe you have a refrigerator full of items but no idea what to cook. That's where we come in!

Our application suggests personalized Indian recipes based on the ingredients you already have, eliminating food waste and inspiring culinary creativity in your kitchen.

Whether you're an experienced cook looking to try something new or a beginner wanting to explore Indian cuisine, MakeMyRecipe is your trusted cooking companion.

Powered by AI

How Gemini AI Powers Our Recipe Suggestions

MakeMyRecipe uses Google's advanced Gemini AI to analyze your available ingredients and suggest the perfect Indian recipes for you.

The Gemini AI model has been trained on thousands of authentic Indian recipes, cooking techniques, and ingredient combinations. When you input your ingredients, our AI analyzes:

  • Traditional Indian flavor profiles and spice combinations
  • Regional cooking techniques from across India
  • Ingredient substitutions and complementary flavors
  • Cooking times and preparation methods

This allows us to provide you with recipes that are not only delicious but also authentic to Indian culinary traditions.

Benefits of AI-Powered Recipes

  • Personalized to your available ingredients
  • Adapts to what you have, not what you don't
  • Suggests creative combinations you might not have considered
  • Learns from user interactions to improve recommendations
  • Provides detailed instructions tailored to your needs
