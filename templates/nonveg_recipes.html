Recipe Title

30 mins

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan, add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides.
  5. Add 1 cup of water, cover and simmer until chicken is tender, about 20 minutes.
  6. Add cream and garam masala. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
